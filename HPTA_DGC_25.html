Is your team performing at its best?

This research-based instrument helps team leaders assess their teams across 14 dimensions drawn from doctoral research into high-performing secondary school teams. It will give you a clear picture of where your team is strong and where it has room to grow.

Complete the assessment and you will receive an initial snapshot of your team's profile. Your results will also be shared with Dr Daniel Groenewald, who will be in touch to discuss what they mean and how to act on them. You are welcome to follow up with him at any time following the assessment.

🏆

Remember — school leadership is a team sport. You can't go it alone.

What this gives you

  • A clear picture of where your team is strong
  • An honest view of where it needs work
  • A research-based framework — not guesswork

What comes next

  • A debrief with Daniel to unpack your results
  • Coaching tailored to your team's specific profile

  • Growth tracked over time — not a one-off exercise

Your Next Step

A debrief is your next step — to understand how you and the team are performing, and what you can do to move performance from good to great. Equally, professional development that addresses gaps in team performance will ensure the team learns from its data and improves.

Next Steps
Team development takes time

High-performing teams, like good relationships, require nurture, and take time to fully bloom. Nurturing a high-performing team is an act of cultivation that emerges from intentional analysis, and the growth and development of the team together.

The most effective teams revisit their data frequently, at key moments in the school year, to track genuine progress, celebrate growth, and refocus where it matters most. Your results today are the beginning, not the destination.
