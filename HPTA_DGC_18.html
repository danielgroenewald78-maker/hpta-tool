Is your team performing at its best?

This is a research-based instrument for helping team leaders to assess their teams. It is based on my research into high-performing teams in secondary schools. It will give you an insight into where your team is strong and where it needs work.

Take the assessment to find out — and please feel welcome to reach out for coaching, or professional development with your team.

🏆

Remember — school leadership is a team sport. You can't go it alone.

What this gives you

  • A clear picture of where your team is strong
  • An honest view of where it needs work
  • A research-based framework — not guesswork
  • A starting point for a real conversation

What comes next

  • A debrief with Daniel to unpack your results
  • Coaching tailored to your team's specific profile
  • Professional development that addresses real gaps
  • Growth tracked over time — not a one-off exercise
