About This Assessment

Before working with a team, we need to understand its strengths and areas for growth. This tool maps your team against 70 research-based items across 14 dimensions, giving us the insight to design a development process that is targeted, evidence-informed, and directly relevant to your context.

High-performing teams don't emerge by chance. Like elite athletes, team members need to train together — not just individually. This assessment is the first step in a structured process that addresses how your team works together, not just what each member brings.

What we do together

  • Purposeful team formation with clear expectations and a team charter
  • Explore the characteristics of high-performing teams (Groenewald et al., 2025)
  • Build coaching and courageous conversation skills

What you can expect

  • Improved collaboration and team cohesion
  • Increased engagement and shared ownership
  • Enhanced decision-making and collective efficacy
